Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Welcome to Minutos! We take your privacy seriously and want you to feel secure when using our services. Here's a clear overview of how we handle your information:

Information We Collect

When you use Minutos, we collect the following types of information:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Task details and timestamps for time tracking
  • Device information, including type and operating system

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide and improve the Minutos service, personalize your experience, and communicate with you. Rest assured, your time tracking data is securely stored, and we do not share it with third parties unless required by law.

Data Storage and Security

Your time tracking data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and alteration.

Third-Party Services

Minutos may integrate with third-party services, such as cloud storage or collaboration tools. If you choose to use these integrations, please be aware that your data may be subject to the privacy policies of those services.

User Controls

At Minutos, we believe in giving you control. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. We provide features to help you manage your data within the app.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

To keep you informed, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our data practices. Rest assured, users will be notified of any significant changes.

Contact Information

Your questions and concerns matter to us. If you have any inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us at contact@minutos.com.
